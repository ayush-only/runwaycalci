Survival Playbook
  • 🔥 Extend by cutting. Reducing burn by 20% often adds 2–3 months of runway — faster than raising.
  • ⚡ Start raising at 6 months. A fundraising round takes 3–6 months to close. Never start too late.
  • 📈 Revenue is the best runway. Even $5K MRR meaningfully extends how long you can operate.
  • 🎯 Target 18+ months. Investors want to see you have time to hit milestones, not just survive.
